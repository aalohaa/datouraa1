S E R V I C E S

Affordable hotels

Coming to the Republic of Kazakhstan, you can not be afraid to be homeless. Even the most fastidious guest of this hospitable region will always have a large selection of places to stay. There are enough hotels in Kazakhstan for any guest. The Republic of Kazakhstan will pleasantly surprise you with an abundance of luxury hotels.

Food and drinks

The cities of Kazakhstan are rich in establishments of different price categories, from expensive steakhouses to small inexpensive pastry shops. Every resident of Kazakhstan or guest will be able to choose something to their liking.

Safety guide

Kazakhstan is a huge country the size of Western Europe, so to understand how to get there, you first need to decide which point you will go to. There are 16 international airports in Kazakhstan, the most important of them are Nursultan Nazarbayev (TSE) and Almaty (ALA). How to get to them is described in detail in the guides for Nursultan and Almaty.

Around the Kazakhstan

For navigation and walking around the city in Kazakhstan, the 2GIS application is used. In almost every city, you can move around using these maps.

Fastest travel

You can travel around Kazakhstan by buses, trains and planes. The flight from Astana to Almaty will take one hour and 40 minutes and will cost an average of 15.5 thousand tenge. .

Adventures

A trip to Kazakhstan is a real adventure full of amazing natural landscapes, the hospitality of local residents and the variety of tastes of national cuisine. This country, located at the junction of Europe and Asia, has absorbed the trends of a variety of cultural and religious trends.
